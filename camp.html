CAMPS ATTENDED BY SRI SAIRAM ENGINEERING COLLEGE CADETS

RDC-REPUBLIC DAY CAMP

The Annual Republic Day Camp (RDC) is held at Garrison Parade Ground, Delhi Cantt every year from 01 Jan to 29 Jan. 2070 selected NCC cadets from all States and Union Territories attend the camp. RDC consists of Best Cadet, Kartavya Path, Culturals, PM Rally, Flag Area, Aeromodelling, Ship modelling events.

TSC-THAL SAINIK CAMP

The TSC is a 12 days camp conducted by NCC in Delhi every year, in which the cadets are selected from all 17 directorates, by the selection procedure conducting 10 TSC selection camps . This camp is specific to the Army Wing of NCC. TSC consists of Map Reading, Obstacle Traning, Firing, FCBC and Health and Hygiene events.

VSC-VAYU SAINIK CAMP

Vayu Sainik Camp. This camp, organised for Air Wing cadets, is attended by 600 cadets from 16 Directorates. VSC is generally conducted in the month of Oct and Inter Directorate competitions pertaining to air wing training are held during this camp. VSC consists of Firing, Flying,Drill, Aero Modelling and Tent Pitching events.

ALC-ADVANCE LEADERSHIP CAMP

Six Advance Leadership Camps (ALC), are conducted every year, which a total of 1350 cadets attend to focus on personality development, leadership skills and orientation for induction into the Armed Forces.

BLC-BASIC LEADERSHIP CAMP

NCC has given us opportunities to take part in various activities and camps, where the cadets were provided training that helped them find their own hidden capabilities & talents one of these camps was the Basic Leadership Camp.

EBSB-EK BHARAT SHRESHTHA BHARAT

EBSB stands for “Ek Bharat Shreshtha Bharat”. In NCC (National Cadet Corp) the EBSB camp held annually unity, nationalism among the cadets, national integration and developing skill.

RCTC-ROCK CLIMBING TRAINING CAMP

Rock Climbing Training Camps (RCTC). Eight rock climbing camps are held each year to expose NCC cadets to the basics of rock climbing and to inculcate a spirit of adventure.

AAC - ARMY ATTACHMENT CAMP

Army attachment camps (AAC) are conducted by the NCC in collaboration with the Indian Army. In this camp, the cadets are trained in military tactics including day and night warfare and also get familiar with the weaponry. AAC has different camps conducted by various regiments. IAC(INFANTRY ATTACHMENTCAMP), AAC(ARTILLERY ATTACHMENT CAMP).

AAC - AIR FORCE ATTACHMENT CAMP

Air Force Attachment camp aims to provide practical training to NCC cadets on various aspects of air force operations and activities.During the camp, cadets are trained in basic airmanship, aircraft recognition, air traffic control, ground duties, and other important aspects of air force operations. They also get an opportunity to interact with air force personnel and learn about their experiences and careers.The attachment camp is usually held for a period of two weeks and is open to NCC cadets who have completed their basic training.

ALL INDIA GIRLS TREKKING CAMP

The All India Girls Trekking Camp in NCC is an annual event organised by the National Cadet Corps of India. The camp aims to provide an opportunity for girls from all over the country to come together and explore the natural beauty of India through trekking. The camp is usually held for a duration of 10 to 15 days, and the trekking route may vary each year. The participants are typically in the age group of 14 to 25 years and are selected from different NCC units across India.

IGC- INTER GROUP COMPETITION

IGC (Inter Group Competition) Camp in NCC (National Cadet Corps) is usually held for a period of two weeks and involves various activities such as drills, adventure sports, and cultural events. Cadets are divided into different groups, and each group competes against the other in various activities.

FIRING CAMP

Range firing is an important and compulsory training for all the NCC cadets. This is done so that the fear of firearms can be removed from the minds of the cadets.This camp is the basis for mavalankar firing camp conducted across the country.
