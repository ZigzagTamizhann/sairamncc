ACADEMIC LEARNING FROM NCC

NCC is a period of 3 year right from your first year of college to third year. In this duration of course one attends many camps in different part of the country. The cadet is prepared to be strong mentally and physically. From the start cadets are taught how to present themselves in front of a group of people. Cadets are taught to speak in English and attend interviews which inturn helps them to prepare for other interviews in their life. Cadets get the chance to meet many defence officers and learn from their experiences and difficulties. Cadets practise punctuality and discipline which improves their lifestyle from before. Cadets are given the opportunity to socialise in camps. They come across different cadets and have to stay with them for the camp and in such situation, they learn the importance of socialising and mingling with cadets. They come out of their comfort zone and step towards learning. The academic curriculum for Air Wing cadets in NCC includes a range of subjects related to aviation and aerospace, such as aeronautics, aircraft systems,aeromodelling,aviation meteorology, air navigation, and aviation safety.The Air Wing cadets are given an opportunity to learn about the Virus SW 80 ‘Garud’ Microlight aircraft. Virus SW microflight is a type of ultralight aircraft that is used for recreational flying and sports purposes..As part of their theoretical learning, Airwing cadets may study the basic concepts of aircraft design, including the various types of aircraft available, their components, and their functionality. .In addition to theoretical learning, Air Wing cadets may also have the opportunity to gain practical experience with ultralight aircraft, including Virus SW microflight. This may include hands-on training in piloting, navigation, and maintenance of the aircraft.
