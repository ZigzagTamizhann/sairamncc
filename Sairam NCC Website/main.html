ABOUT SAIRAM NCC

NCC Air wing for senior division boys and senior wing girls was started in Sir Sai Ram Engineering College in September 2002. It comes under the 1(TN) AIR SQN NCC,Madras B.The NCC Army Wing soon came to function in the year 2018.It comes under 1(TN) BTY NCC, Madras A Group. Ever since its inception both the wings has been functioning extremely well with the constant and motivating support from the management thereby bringing laurels to the unit as well as the college management through various achievements. The vacancies for both the wings are 50.
